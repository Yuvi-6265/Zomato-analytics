# 🍽️ Zomato Restaurant Data Analysis | Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on performing **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on the Zomato Restaurant dataset to identify the key factors influencing restaurant ratings and customer preferences. The analysis explores restaurant locations, cuisines, pricing, online services, customer reviews, and other features to uncover actionable business insights.

The project helps restaurant owners, food enthusiasts, and businesses understand the characteristics of highly rated restaurants through data-driven analysis.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Explore and understand the Zomato restaurant dataset.
* Analyze restaurant ratings and customer preferences.
* Identify trends based on location, cuisine, pricing, and services.
* Discover factors associated with highly rated restaurants.
* Create meaningful visualizations to communicate insights.

## 💡 Key Business Insights

* Restaurant ratings vary significantly across cities and cuisines.
* Popular cuisines tend to receive more customer engagement.
* Online ordering and table booking are associated with better customer convenience.
* Pricing influences customer perception but does not always determine higher ratings.
* Restaurant amenities such as Wi-Fi and delivery services contribute positively to customer experience.
